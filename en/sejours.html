Diving in the heart of the calanques

4/5 4.86 - 6 days - 18 à 35 years old Piana, Ajaccio, Corse

Share

Summary of the stay:

"Discover the calanques of Piana, with their granite rocks, their extraordinary colors and their clear waters. Go diving at the foot of the cliffs and observe a great diversity of Mediterranean fish: bream, girelles, groupers, morays, ... In this protected reserve, you will appreciate the Mediterranean sweetness and its unique heritage."

The advantages of the stay:

  • Fine sandy beach in direct proximity
  • Clear and warm water
  • Intensive dive program with 6 dives
  • Diving club at 200 m from the accommodation

Program of the stay :

  • Arrival day: welcome at Ajaccio airport at 10am and transportation by bus to the center. Hike to the "tête de chien" to appreciate the spectacular views of the calanques.
  • Days 2 to 4: 2 daily dives on the most beautiful dive sites of the creeks with the club's boat.
  • Day 5: Hiking in the Ayton forest with swimming in the pools of the torrents in the morning, mountain biking on the forest roads
  • Day 6: Swimming on the beach of Porto and meal tasting of local products (cold cuts, cheese, chestnut cream). Departure to the airport at 6 pm.
